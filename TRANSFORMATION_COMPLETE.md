## 📊 Neural Pathways Explained

### Right Hand → Left Motor Cortex
```
1. Touch sensor on right hand (0.35, -0.35, 0)
2. Signal travels through wrist
3. Up forearm (median nerve)
4. Past elbow joint
5. Through upper arm (humerus region)
6. To shoulder (brachial plexus)
7. Enters spinal cord at C5
8. Ascends to C3 level
9. Reaches brainstem
10. Crosses to left side (thalamus) ← CONTRALATERAL!
11. Activates LEFT motor cortex (-0.08, 1.52, 0)

Total: 11 waypoints, 1.87 units distance, 1.5 seconds
```

### Eye → Occipital Lobe
```
1. Photoreceptors in retina (0, 1.75, 0.15)
2. Optic nerve (CN II) entry
3. Optic chiasm (partial crossing)
4. Lateral geniculate nucleus (thalamus)
5. Primary visual cortex (0, 1.47, -0.08)

Total: 5 waypoints, 0.33 units distance, 1.5 seconds
```
